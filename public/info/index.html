marcello maranzan (b. 1999) is a photographer based between Pordenone and Milano. after the degree in photography and visual arts at IED Milano he drew his attention to architecture photography. he's currently working as a freelance taking care of printed matter productions, location scoutings and projects in regard to architecture and design. his interest lies in the intersection of landscape and human infrastructures, often involving a priori visual research.
